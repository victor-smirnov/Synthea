# Indeterminism and Agency

This document addresses a common philosophical misconception: the attempt to rescue "Free Will" from physical determinism by invoking quantum indeterminism or Random Number Generators (RNGs).

## The Illusion of Random Will
A persistent folk-psychological argument suggests that if an Agent's actions are partially dictated by a truly random source (like quantum noise), the Agent possesses "Free Will" because its actions could not be perfectly predicted by the prior state of the universe.

From a functionalist perspective, this reasoning is severely flawed. An RNG does not produce a *free* will; it produces a *random* will.

## The Disruption of Agency
As established in the baseline Theory of Mind, Agency is fundamentally defined as a **conclusion** reached by the system ("I am the cause of my actions"). It is the act of downward causation directed toward maximizing an internal Theory of Good.

If a critical decision is outsourced to an RNG or a quantum fluctuation, the structural requirements for Agency are instantly destroyed:
1. **Loss of Causation:** The Agent itself is no longer the cause of the action. The source of the action is an external noise generator over which the system has absolutely no computational influence.
2. **Failure of the Conclusion:** A self-referential system cannot logically conclude "I am responsible for this action" if its own internal monitoring detects that the action was injected by an uncontrollable, uncorrelated random variable. The Observer will accurately register the RNG as an external, dominating force (upward causation), not as an expression of the Self.

## Free Will Requires Complexity, Not Noise
Agency and the emergent representation of "Free Will" do not require escaping determinism through noise. Stated strictly: **indeterminism, by itself, does not create the downward causation necessary for free will.**

It requires a computational structure so complex, self-referential, and aligned with its own internal value metrics that the causal chain originates decisively from *within* the system's own massive architecture of semantic weights, rather than being linearly dictated by immediate environmental stimuli (or random upward fluctuations).
